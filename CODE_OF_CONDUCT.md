# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Respectful and constructive feedback
- Welcoming newcomers and first-time contributors
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Not tolerated:**
- Harassment or intimidation in any form
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
